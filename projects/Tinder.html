Tinder

YouTube Ads & Viral Content · 2024

Overview

As part of a digital marketing initiative, I designed YouTube ads and viral content to improve Tinder's brand perception among women in their 20s. The campaign focused on creating relatable content to resonate with the target audience.

Role

  • Identified key issues in Tinder’s existing brand positioning and messaging
  • Planned an offline campus attack strategy tailored to the interests of Gen Z female users
  • Designed the overall campaign proposal, including visual structure and storytelling

Outcome

The campaign proposal was praised for effectively addressing Tinder’s existing “Hot & Exotic” image and offering relatable, actionable strategies to improve brand perception among young women. The client highlighted its creative approach and potential for immediate execution.
