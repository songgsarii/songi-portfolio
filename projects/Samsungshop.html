Samsung Shop

ASO & Localization Support · 2024

Overview

As an intern, I participated in the Samsung Shop ASO (App Store Optimization) project. The goal was to improve app visibility and performance across 32 countries through ASO analysis and localization support.

Role

  • Structured and analyzed raw app performance data to uncover insights
  • Conducted keyword research to enhance app discoverability in local markets
  • Supported localization across 32 countries, ensuring cultural and linguistic alignment

Outcome

The project contributed to improvements in app visibility and user engagement across multiple regions. Insights from keyword analysis and A/B testing were applied to refine store strategies and boost download conversion performance.
