About Me

I'm Songi Og. A marketer, maker and strategist.
I’m currently studying English Literature and Business Administration at Ewha Womans University, where I’ve developed a deep appreciation for storytelling, critical thinking, and strategic communication. Throughout university, I’ve actively participated in multiple student-led academic clubs and industry collaboration projects. I served as a team leader in the Marketing & PR club EPRIS, where I led projects with brands like 3M Korea, Uber, and Tinder, focusing on brand positioning, customer journey mapping, and digital campaign ideation.
These experiences helped me realize how much I enjoy blending creativity with logic — and that’s what led me to digital marketing. Recently, I’ve been diving deeper into HTML/CSS and studying digital marketing analytics, particularly CRM strategies and App Store Optimization. I'm fascinated by how data and design come together to drive real impact. Whether it’s crafting a marketing strategy or building a portfolio from scratch, I’m passionate about creating things that are both thoughtful and visually meaningful.
